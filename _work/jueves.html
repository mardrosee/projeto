--- layout: workdetail title: "Jueves" year: "2015" where: "Facultad de Bellas Artes, Universidad Complutense de Madrid" intro: "Jueves is a group project developed for a video class in our senior year of college. This event changed the city forever and is now known as 11M. It was made in collaboration with Paula Frutos, Victor Reverter and Sara Giralt." ---
